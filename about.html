Meet our Instructors

Roger Gracier

Roger Gracie is 4th Degree black belt and a 10 time world IBJJF Champion and is considered by many as the GOAT. He has been a black belt since 2003. Roger Gracie is also an expert in wrestling and MMA having had 10 progessional MMA fights.

Dan Strauss

Dan Strauss is black belt under Roger Gracier and a A British and English champion at black belt, NAGA elite European champion and a number one ranked black belt by the UKBJJA. He's been invited to and competed in the world's biggest submission grappling tournaments such as Polaris pro, ADCC and the Eddie Bravo Invitational
