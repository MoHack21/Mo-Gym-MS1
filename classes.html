Classes

Discover and learn the fine martial art of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu that revolves around grappling and ground fighting. Most of the techniques focus on taking your opponent to the ground where you can control and look to submit them – they tap out.

Adults

BJJ promotes the idea that a smaller, weaker person can use leverage, grip, skill, position plus tactical skills and problem solving to successfully defend against a bigger, stronger assailant by successfully applying a range of techniques like chokeholds and joint locks.

Kids

Our advanced BJJ classes are for 4-16 year olds, taught by a professional GI and are friendly and educational. Our classes give children the chance to make new friends and learn new skills. They also see the importance of working with others and respecting fellow students’ needs and abilities.
